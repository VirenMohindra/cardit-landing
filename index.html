Manoj Adlakha, Founder

Manoj Adlakha, former SVP & Head International Marketing at American Express, resigned after 30 years of service. Adlakha is a CA, holds a BCom Hons from SRCC, and completed an Executive Leadership Program at Duke University. Between 2005-2009, he worked at Visa and Yes Bank. He rejoined Amex as CEO from 2014 to Q1 2022, launching premium cards and leading Amex India. Known for his strategic, customer-focused approach and strong leadership, Adlakha contributed to Amex becoming India's Most Respected Credit Brand for eight years and received personal accolades for his industry impact.
